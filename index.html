Malaysian Tourism Trends (2010-2020)

This visualization explores various aspects of Malaysian tourism, including visitor arrivals, popular destinations, and economic impact.

The map above shows the distribution of tourist arrivals across different states in Malaysia. Kuala Lumpur, Penang, and Sabah are among the most visited areas.

This chart displays the trend of tourist arrivals in Malaysia from 2010 to 2020. Note the significant drop in 2020 due to the global COVID-19 pandemic.

Top Source Countries

Malaysia attracts tourists from various countries, with Singapore, Indonesia, and China being the top sources. The proximity of Singapore and cultural similarities with Indonesia contribute to their high numbers.

Tourism Revenue

Tourism is a significant contributor to Malaysia's economy. This chart shows the tourism revenue over the years, highlighting its importance to the country's economic growth.

Popular Tourist Activities

Malaysia offers a diverse range of activities for tourists. Shopping, beach activities, and cultural experiences are among the most popular. The country's rich biodiversity also attracts nature lovers and adventure seekers.
